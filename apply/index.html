// The fine print, in plain language

Health first. The application includes a short health screening. Some answers mean I'll ask for a quick OK from your physician before we start — that's not a rejection, it's how this is done properly.

Commitment. Coached and All-In carry a 3-month minimum, because that's the honest timeframe for real change. Self-Guided is one-time with a 14-day refund.

Capacity. I coach a limited roster on purpose — All-In is capped at 8 clients. If spots are full, the application becomes a waitlist and I'll tell you exactly where you stand.
